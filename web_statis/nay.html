Departments and Programs

UKM Bahasa-FLAT board is comprised of several department. Firts, Human Resource Department which has many program; one of which is Foreign Language Orientation (FLO). The program us designed to provide basic foreign language and organization skills orientation for UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta's students who have passed selection process before they become UKM Bahasa-FLAT's members....

Second, Language Department. Foreign languages classes are organized by this department to improve the member's foreign languages skills of English, Arabic, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, French and German.

Third, Skills Development Department that concerns on various skills development related to foreign languages. There are many programs in this department; one of which is FLAT Debate center, which consist of debate workshop, debate sparring, and devate competition. This program is provide to imrprove UKM Bahasa-FLAT's members debate skills in English and Arabic. In addition, it was purposed to more systematic debate training.

Next, General Administration Department which focuses on UKM Bahasa-FLAT's inventory aspects. Not only that, there is also an internal-based application for member's database. This application is used to record UKM Bahasa-FLAT's inventory and the data of UKM Bahasa-FLAT's members. This department is more responsible for oraganizing the secretariat office of UKM Bahasa-FLAT.

Last, Public Relation Department. One of its program is ICFest (International Culture Festival). It's an event held to celebrate UKM Bahasa-FLAT's anniversary as well as to introduce various cultures both local and foreign cultures to UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta's environment.
