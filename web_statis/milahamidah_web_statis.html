Tentang Kami

Unit Kegiatan Mahasiswa Bahasa-Foreign Languages Association (UKM Bahasa-FLAT) is a student activity unit existing in UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta whose spotlight is, as the name suggests, on foreign languages. UKM Bahasa-FLAT is an association for people who have great interest on foreign languages especially those who want to learn and to share knowledge about them. We also focus on the realm of culture, both Indonesian and foreign cultures
